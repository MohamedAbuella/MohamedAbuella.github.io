This project provides an interactive tool for assessing the long-term financial viability of energy technologies through NPV and payback period analysis.

Interactive GB Energy Generation NPV & Payback Calculator

Techno-Economic Assessment of Energy Technologies: Calculations of NPV & Payback

ID Type Max GW (2025) Max GW (2050) Op Costs (£/MWh) Capex (£/MW) [ε] Fixed Cost Frac [φ] Econ Life (yrs) [n] Emissions (tCO2/MWh) [h] Discount Rate [r] Capacity Factor [CF] Invest (£m) Ann. Cash Flow (£m) NPV (£m) Payback (yrs)

Notes & Assumptions:

  • **Interactivity:** Parameter cells (light blue background: Max GW (2025) through CF) are editable. Click to edit, press Enter or click outside to confirm. ID and Type are not editable.
  • **Calculations:** Financial results (Investment, Cash Flow, NPV, Payback) are calculated based on the editable **'Max GW (2050)'** value (which is converted internally to MW). NPV calculation uses the 'Econ Life (yrs)'. **Payback period calculation ignores the 'Econ Life (yrs)' constraint** and reports the theoretical time to recover investment if mathematically possible, otherwise 'Never'.
  • **Global Controls:** Avg. Electricity Price and Carbon Price apply to all technologies. Changing these triggers a recalculation for all rows.
  • **Set All Fixed Cost Frac (φ):** Use the input and 'Apply φ' button to override the 'Fixed Cost Frac \[φ]' column for all technologies simultaneously. Individual cells in that column can still be edited afterwards.
  • Investment is assumed upfront (Year 0).
  • Revenue, Operational Costs (Variable & Fixed), and Carbon Costs are assumed constant over the economic lifetime.
  • Annual Fixed Cost = Cell's φ * Cell's ε * (Cell's Max GW (2050) * 1000).
  • Annual Variable Cost = Annual Generation * Op Costs (£/MWh).
  • Annual Carbon Cost = Annual Generation * Emissions * Carbon Price.
  • Annual Generation (MWh) = (Cell's Max GW (2050) * 1000) * CF * 8760 (hours/year).
  • NPV = Present Value of (Annual Revenue - Annual Var Cost - Annual Fixed Cost - Annual Carbon Cost) over 'n' years - Initial Investment.
  • Payback Period is the discounted time for cumulative cash flows to recover the initial investment. 'Never' indicates payback is mathematically impossible given cash flow and discount rate, or cash flow is non-positive. 'N/A' if no capacity.
  • NPV, Investment and Annual Cash Flow are shown in millions (£m). All numeric values rounded to 2 decimal places, except Econ Life (integer) and Capex (integer).
  • Storage, P2G, G2G revenue/cost models are simplified; real-world operation is more complex.
